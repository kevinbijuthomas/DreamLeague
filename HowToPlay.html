How To Play

What is Dreamleague?
Build a team of Premier League football players, score points based on their weekly real life performances!
How do packs work?
Start your journey by opening 'packs' that will give you fifteen players from the Premier League to complete your first squad. Every week, on the day before the start of the first Premier League game you will be given the chance to open another pack to replace one member of your squad.
Players are classed in to three ranks based on past season performances: gold, silver or bronze.
What is the scoring system?

- Goals scored = 10
- Assists = 5
- Red cards = -5
- Yellow cards = -2
- Clean sheets (goalkeeper and defenders) = 10
- Appearance = 3
- Own goals = -10
